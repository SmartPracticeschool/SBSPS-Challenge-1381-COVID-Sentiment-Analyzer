COVID Pandemic is not a word to use lightly or carelessly

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. COVID-19 is now a pandemic affecting many countries globally.

The COVID-19 pandemic in India is part of the worldwide pandemic of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The first case of COVID-19 in India, which originated from China, was reported on 30 January 2020. As of 21 June 2020, the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MoHFW) has confirmed a total of 410,461 cases, 227,756 recoveries (including 1 migration) and 13,254 deaths in the country.

OUR WEBPAGE CONTAINS

tweet Visualizer

Dashboard for sentimental analysis of coronavirus tweets and lockdown extension

In brief , we have created an user interface that will retrieve the most recent tweets of people from that country and perform sentiment analysis on those tweets and display the results in IBM Cognos Dashboard. The user can know these details from domains like Twitter, Google and ET News by applying filters.

COVID stats

Worldwide Statistics Dashboard to visualise the covid19 updates

The statistics dashboard will contain the number of cases confirmed ,numbers of deaths confirmed and people recovered from each state of India and also different countries of the world. The user can know these details for each region by applying filters and these data are visualized using Dashboard.
